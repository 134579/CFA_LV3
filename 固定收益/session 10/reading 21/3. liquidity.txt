唯一考点
影响liquidity的因素
1. liquidity
	1. 特点
		1. bond liquidity < equity liquidity
		2. on the run bond liquidity > off the run liquidity
			新债券比老债券流动性好
		3. liquidity ↓ -> risk ↓ -> return ↑
		4. goverment bond liquidity > corporate bond
			原因:
			1. issuance size 大
			2. used as benchmark 
			3. used as collateral(担保) in repo(逆回购)
			4. corporate bond has high credit risk
			
	2. 影响liquidity的因素
		1. issuer
			goverment >corporate
		2. credit
			↑ => liquidity ↑, 信用高好发
		3. issue frequency
			↑ => liquidity ↑, 老面孔好发债
		4. issue size
			↑ => liquidity ↑, 发行数量大, 便于做市
		5. maturity
			short term => liquidity ↑, 短期债券风险小些
