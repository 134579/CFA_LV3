1. foundation, endowment区别
	1. 区别
		foundation				endowment
		个人						组织
		CF单一						CF diversify
		监管更严					监管更松
		强制spending rule			不强制spending rule
	
	2. spending rule
		每年必须把资产的一部分做慈善, 才能免税
		
2. foundation
	1. 分类
		individual foundation: 个人设立
		company sponsored foundation: 公司成立
		operating foundation: 为某一个目的设立的 foundation
		community foundation: 和endowment一样
		
3. rrttllu
	1. return
		1. objective
			1. indefinitely long horizen
				perpetual support 永续存在
			2. preserve the real value
				maintain the real value 抗通胀
				
		2. 计算
			required rate of return = spending rate + inflation rate + (fund expense)
			fund expense可能有, 可能没有
			inflation以资助的行业inflation为主, 如果没有才用整个国家的inflation rate
	
	2. risk tolerance
		1. 比较foundation和pension
