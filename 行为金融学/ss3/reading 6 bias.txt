1. behavioral bias
	1. cognitive error
		1. belief perseverance bias
			1. conservatism bias, 保守偏见
				maintain their prior view
				
			2. confirmation bias, 确认偏见
				只注意肯定他们观点的证据
				only notice information that agrees with their perception
				ignoring evidence that don't agree with their view or decision 
			
			3. representativeness bias, 代表性偏见
				以偏概全
				sample size neglect			
				
			4. illusion of control
				以为自己能控制
				tend to believe they can control or influnce the result when in fact they can't
			
			5. hindsight bias
				事后诸葛亮
				thought past things was predictable
				
		2. information processing bias
			1. anchoring and adjustment, 锚定效应
				估计某个值时, 莫名其妙脑补一个初始点, 然后根据这个锚来调整..
			2. mental account bias, 心理账户
				在心里把相同数量的钱分给两个心理账户, 给与不同的风险偏好
			3. framing bias, 
				问问题的不同方法导致结果不同
			4. availability bias,
				用下意识的念头来判断
				
	2. emotional bias
		harder to correct
		1. loss aversion bias
		2. overconfidence bias
			1. prediction overconfidence
			2. self attribution bias
				成功归自己, 失败怪他人
				self protection bias
				self enhancing bias
			3. self control bias
				控制不了自己去干长期有益的事
				lack of self discipline
			4. satatus quo bias
				保持现状
				do nothing instead of making changes
			5. endowment bias
				对自己拥有的东西过高估值
			6. regret aversion bias
				后悔厌恶
				心血来潮做一个新决策, 然后损失了, 后悔感比通常的决策损失更强烈
